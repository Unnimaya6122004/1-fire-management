Precautions for Domestic Fires

Fires at home can be devastating. Here are some precautions you can take to prevent domestic fires and ensure the safety of your family and property:

  • Install smoke alarms on every floor of your home and test them monthly.

  • Keep flammable items away from heat sources and open flames.

  • Regularly check and maintain electrical wiring and appliances.

  • Store matches and lighters out of children's reach.

  • Never leave cooking unattended, especially when using the stove or deep fryer.

  • Have a fire extinguisher in your kitchen and learn how to use it.

  • Develop a fire escape plan with your family and practice it regularly.

  • Teach your children about the dangers of fire and what to do in case of a fire emergency.

  • Properly dispose of cigarettes and ashes, especially in dry and windy conditions.

  • Keep space heaters at least three feet away from anything that can burn.
